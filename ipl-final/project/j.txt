Monday 23, March 2009 

 
Kolkata Knight Riders added five more young cricketes to the already announced contingent of 28. Shoiab Sheikh {Mumbai}, Sunny Singh {Haryana} Arindam Ghosh and Sourav Sarkar {both Bengal} and Anureet Singh {Railways} are the new cricketers. 

Monish Parmar, the Gujarat Offie, who is under rehabilitation programme at National Cricket Academy, Bangalore would be included in the team after he gets clearance from the proper authority, according to John Buchanan, Director of Cricket Operations of the franchise. The team would be pruned to 22 in two week’s time
